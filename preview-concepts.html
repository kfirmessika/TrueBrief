Concept A — "Intel Briefing"

Dark mode. Authority. Like a classified brief or Bloomberg terminal.
Psychology: makes the user feel informed, serious, expert. High signal-to-noise.

Concept B — "Pulse"

Clean white. Left importance bar = how big is this story. Like a vitals monitor or Notion inbox.
Psychology: the eye immediately goes to the orange bar = "this one matters". Calm but alive.

Concept C — "Chapters"

A story that keeps updating. Each brief = a chapter. New ones are open, old ones collapse.
Psychology: feels like you're reading a living book. The green number = "you're on chapter 3". Very satisfying to scroll back and see history stack up.
